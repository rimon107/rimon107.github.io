**BucketKD: A Safety-Aware Bucket-Based Knowledge Distillation Framework for End-to-End Motion Planning** is my M.S. thesis project at the University of Memphis.

BucketKD focuses on compressing end-to-end autonomous-driving planners while preserving safety-relevant planning behavior. The project introduces bucket-based knowledge distillation and time-to-collision-guided waypoint attention for safety-aware motion-planner compression.

### Highlights

- Developed a bucket-based knowledge-distillation framework for end-to-end motion-planner compression.
- Designed adaptive planning-state discretization to preserve safety-relevant trajectory behavior.
- Developed time-to-collision (TTC)-guided waypoint attention for safety-aware distillation.
- Evaluated compact student planners in high-complexity CARLA driving scenarios.
- Submitted to IROS 2026.
