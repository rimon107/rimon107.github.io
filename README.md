# Hi, I'm Md Nahidul Islam 

I am a **Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Memphis**, advised by **Dr. Myounggyu Won**. My research focuses on building **trustworthy and efficient autonomous systems**, especially in:

- Autonomous driving and interactive motion planning
- Trajectory forecasting and counterfactual evaluation
- Conformal risk certification for safe robotics
- Model compression and knowledge distillation
- Applied machine learning for real-world systems
